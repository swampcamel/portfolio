About Myself

For the past 5 years, I have been active in business, design, and startups.

Born in Sacramento and raised in Utah, I pursued a degree in Film Studies at the University of Utah, but after feeling an outsider in the industry, I turned my attention and efforts toward my other passions--coding languages and graphic design.

I specialize in designing brand identity and website development. I don’t restrict myself to design. The blend of design, marketing, and business theory is where I feel I excel. Design should translate into economic value for the products/services of your business.

Through research and insight into the audiences your business is serving, I can help to identify the values of your target clients. Then, by developing branded aesthetics and the right marketing strategy, your audience will enjoy the best user experience.

During my career, I have taught myself to code in front-end languages. This allows me to put my marketing vision in place exactly as I have it in my head. I know the possibilities and restrictions of user interfaces and I know how to operate in teams.

I recently have started a coding bootcamp at Epicodus in Portland, OR, to advance my skills in web and mobile application development in order to extend my skills toward functional development of great freatures and applications.

On a personal level: I’m a passionate traveler, rock climber, snowboarder and musician. I love to work on side-projects that help enrich communities and others’ personal lives.
